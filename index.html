00 — What is Tabula Rasa
A space to create universes.
A blank surface. An open call. A community that decides who fills it —
by doing real good in the world.

Each Tabula is a portal. Physical and digital.
What begins as a surface can become a comic, a character,
a soundtrack, an entirely new universe.

Universes feed other universes.
We produce the space. Not the art.
01
The blank surface
Real spaces in cities around the world. No rules, given to creators through open global calls.
02
The active community
Voting costs something: real-world good deeds. Volunteering, time, acts of impact. Each action generates tokens to choose the next creator.
03
The local fabric
Local voices carry more weight. Each Tabula belongs to its neighborhood. Alliances with community organizations activate the social tissue.
01 — How we grow
One Tabula at a time.
This is a living project. We build in stages — each phase opens when the previous one is ready.
